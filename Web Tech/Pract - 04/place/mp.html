About Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, located in central India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and diverse geography. It is the second-largest state in India by area and offers a mix of natural beauty and historical significance.

Madhya Pradesh is home to attractions like Khajuraho Group of Monuments, Sanchi Stupa, and national parks like Kanha and Bandhavgarh. It boasts a variety of wildlife, including tigers, and is a paradise for nature enthusiasts.
