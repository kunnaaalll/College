About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India and is known for its vibrant culture, historic landmarks, and economic significance.

Mumbai is home to various attractions, including the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, and the bustling street markets. The city is also the entertainment and financial hub of India.
